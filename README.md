# Brew Theory Coffee Bar Sales Analysis Dashboard ☕📊

An interactive business intelligence project focused on analyzing retail coffee shop sales data to uncover actionable insights, improve operational performance, and support strategic decision-making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes transactional sales data from a coffee shop business using Excel dashboards and data visualization techniques. The objective is to identify sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, peak business hours, product performance, and store-level revenue insights.

The project demonstrates practical skills in:
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Sales Analysis
- Business Intelligence Reporting
- Dashboard Design
- Data Visualization
- KPI Tracking

---

## 📈 Key Business Questions Solved
- How do sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?
- What are the peak sales hours?
- What is the monthly revenue trend?
- Which store location performs best?
- What is the average order value per customer?
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- How do product categories contribute to overall sales?

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Morning hours (8 AM – 10 AM) generate the highest sales volume.
- Coffee products contribute the majority of total revenue.
- June recorded the highest monthly revenue growth.
- Hell’s Kitchen is the top-performing store location.
- Barista Espresso is the best-selling product category.
